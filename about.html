Our Story

MethusBio is a trusted distributor of premium biologics products. We partner with leading laboratories to ensure our products meet the highest standards of quality, consistency, and ethical sourcing. Our mission is to support scientists and innovators in advancing regenerative science by providing the materials they need to unlock groundbreaking discoveries, and to advise on optimizing the manufacturing process.

Our Mission

“To support scientific innovation by providing high-quality, ethically sourced biologics for research use, enabling groundbreaking discoveries in regenerative science.”

Our Vision

“To become the trusted global leader in delivering biologics that empower researchers to unlock the future of regenerative science.”

Our Value Proposition

At MethusBio, we are dedicated to bridging the gap between ethical sourcing and reliable research results.

  • Rigorous Testing: All products undergo comprehensive testing to ensure quality and compatibility with advanced research methodologies.
  • Ethical Sourcing: We adhere to global standards for donor consent and traceability, prioritizing transparency and responsibility in procurement.
  • Research Focused: Designed exclusively for scientific studies, our products support cutting-edge discoveries.

Our Commitment

We believe in empowering researchers to achieve breakthroughs. Whether exploring new frontiers in regenerative science or validating innovative methodologies, MethusBio is your trusted partner for reliable and ethically sourced biologics.

What We Offer

At MethusBio, we specialize in providing rigorously tested biologics for research purposes. Our product offerings include Human Mesenchymal Cells and differentiated cell lines derived from Umbilical Cord sources, such as:

  • hMSC-UC
  • Chondrocytes
  • Cardiomyocytes
  • Neural cells
  • Renal cells
  • And more

Key Features

  • ≥25 million cells per vial (per 0.5ml fluid)
  • Passage 3
  • Pathogen and Contaminant Free
  • Xeno-Free and Immune Privileged

Our products undergo stringent testing for identity, purity, viability, sterility, and potency, ensuring they meet the highest industry standards.
